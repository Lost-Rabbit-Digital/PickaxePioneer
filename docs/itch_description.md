## Full Description (HTML for itch.io editor)

```html
<p><strong>One more row. Always one more row.</strong></p>

<p>
  <em>Pickaxe Pioneer</em> is a cozy 2D mining roguelite about a colony of cats — the Clowder —
  scratching their way deeper into the earth in search of rare gems, buried fossils, and enough
  riches to keep the colony alive. Your energy is finite. The ore is richest at the bottom.
  The exit is a long way above you.
</p>

<p>
  <em>
    If you lost hours to Motherload or Super Motherload wondering what's one row deeper,
    or you love the chill between-run progression of Stardew Valley —
    Pickaxe Pioneer is built for you.
  </em>
</p>

<h2>⛏️ The Loop</h2>
<p>
  Choose a mine from your overworld warren. Descend into a procedurally generated 128-row shaft.
  Mine ore with your cursor — richer veins wait deeper, but every underground tile costs a
  heartbeat of stamina. Bank your haul by reaching the surface Exit Station.
  Run out of energy or HP? Lose everything you dug up. Spend what you've banked on permanent
  Clowder upgrades. Head back down — a little deeper this time.
</p>

<h2>✨ Features</h2>
<ul>
  <li>
    <strong>Procedural mines</strong> — every run is unique, with depth-weighted ore distribution,
    open cave rooms with ore-rich walls, hazard clusters, and energy nodes scattered throughout
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Five boss encounters</strong> — from the Giant Rat King at row 32 to the Ancient Hound
    in the deepest caverns at row 128, each defeated using your existing mining tools —
    no separate combat system
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Scout Cat companion</strong> — your forager automatically collects 40% of nearby ore
    and banks it directly when full, safe from death — a tiny insurance policy on every risky dive
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Smelting chains</strong> — mine the same ore type consecutively to trigger chain bonuses
    and unlock alloy combinations for extra minerals
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Fossil archive</strong> — 50 unique fossils hidden across all depths tell the buried
    history of the earth beneath the Clowder; a forgiveness mechanic ensures even unlucky cats
    uncover them all eventually
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Gem socketing</strong> — socket rare gems mined deep underground into four permanent
    upgrade slots: more HP, faster paws, sharper claws, or wider sonar
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Colony Chambers</strong> — build five passive-bonus rooms in your Clowder hub, each
    unlocked by a gameplay milestone (a boss defeated, a depth reached, fossils found)
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Sonar ping</strong> — fire a whisker-resonance pulse through solid rock to reveal
    nearby ore; radius and energy cost improve with upgrades
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Settlement rest stops</strong> — spend banked minerals before each descent on
    Energy Caches, Pelt Patches, Mining Shrooms, and Claw Whetstones
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Overworld warren</strong> — navigate a node-based map between mines of varying depth,
    richness, and danger; unlock deeper shafts by mastering shallower ones
  </li>
</ul>

<h2>🐱 The Clowder</h2>
<p>
  Between runs you return to the Clowder — your feline civilization's hub. Amber-lit tunnels,
  idle colony cats, and the quiet satisfaction of upgrading your kit with a hard-earned haul.
  Spend banked minerals on four permanent upgrade tracks: thicken your <strong>Pelt</strong>,
  strengthen your <strong>Paws</strong>, sharpen your <strong>Claws</strong>, or refine your
  <strong>Whiskers</strong> for deeper sonar. Build out Colony Chambers for run-wide passive bonuses.
  The Clowder grows with every successful haul you bring home.
</p>

<h2>🎮 Controls</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Move:</strong> WASD or Arrow Keys</li>
  <li><strong>Jump:</strong> W / Up / Space</li>
  <li><strong>Mine:</strong> Left-click (within 4.5 tiles)</li>
  <li><strong>Sonar ping:</strong> Q</li>
  <li><strong>Pheromone marker:</strong> F</li>
  <li><strong>Interact:</strong> E</li>
  <li><strong>Pause:</strong> Esc</li>
</ul>

<h2>🖼️ Art &amp; Sound</h2>
<p>
  Pixel art visuals built on the <strong>Comfy52 palette</strong>. The mine shifts from warm soil
  browns near the surface through cool stone greys and glowing copper veins to deep purples and
  cyan gem formations near the bottom. Cozy ambient tracks keep the mood relaxed — until a boss
  appears.
</p>
```
