## Full Description (HTML for itch.io editor)

```html
<p><strong>Your colony is counting on you. Dig deep — and come back alive.</strong></p>

<p>
  <em>Pickaxe Pioneer</em> is a cozy 2D mining roguelite about a colony of cats scraping through
  procedurally generated mines in search of rare gems and forgotten fossils. Every run sends you
  deeper into the earth. Richer ore, deadlier hazards, and ancient bosses lurk below — but only
  the minerals you carry back to the surface count toward your colony's future.
</p>

<h2>⛏️ The Loop</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Descend into a procedurally generated mine, 128 rows deep.</li>
  <li>Mine ore with your cursor — richer seams reward cats bold enough to go deeper.</li>
  <li>Manage your finite energy. Every underground tile costs a heartbeat of stamina.</li>
  <li>Defeat depth-milestone bosses guarding the rarest veins.</li>
  <li>Escape before your energy runs dry — or lose everything you dug up.</li>
  <li>Bank your haul and upgrade the Clowder for the next run.</li>
</ul>

<h2>🐱 A Colony Built on Claws</h2>
<p>
  Between runs you return to <strong>the Clowder</strong> — your feline civilization's hub.
  Spend banked minerals on permanent upgrades: thicken your pelt, sharpen your claws,
  strengthen your paws, or refine your whiskers for sharper sonar. Build out five colony
  chambers that grant passive bonuses for every run that follows.
</p>

<h2>✨ Features</h2>
<ul>
  <li>
    <strong>Procedural mines</strong> — every dive is unique, with depth-weighted ore
    distribution, cave rooms, hazard clusters, and energy nodes scattered throughout.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Five boss encounters</strong> — from the Giant Rat King at row 32 to the
    Ancient Hound guarding the deepest gem caverns at row 128, each boss demands a
    different strategy using your existing mining tools.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Scout Cat companion</strong> — your forager automatically collects 40% of
    nearby ore and banks it directly, safe from death. A tiny insurance policy on
    every risky run.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Smelting chains</strong> — mine ore of the same type consecutively to trigger
    chain bonuses and unlock alloy combinations for bonus minerals.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Fossil archive</strong> — 50 unique fossils hidden at every depth tell the
    history of the earth beneath the Clowder. A pity mechanic ensures even unlucky
    cats will uncover them all eventually.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Sonar ping</strong> — send out a whisker-resonance pulse to reveal nearby
    ore through solid rock. Radius and energy cost improve with upgrades.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Gem socketing</strong> — socket rare gems into permanent upgrade slots to
    customize your cat's build: more HP, faster paws, sharper claws, or wider sonar.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Settlement consumables</strong> — stock up before each run with energy
    caches, pelt patches, mining shrooms, and claw whetstones.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Overworld map</strong> — navigate a node-based warren between mines of
    varying depth, richness, and danger. Unlock deeper mines by mastering shallower ones.
  </li>
</ul>

<h2>🎮 Controls</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Move:</strong> WASD or Arrow Keys</li>
  <li><strong>Jump:</strong> W / Up / Space</li>
  <li><strong>Mine:</strong> Left-click (within 4.5 tiles)</li>
  <li><strong>Sonar ping:</strong> Q</li>
  <li><strong>Pheromone marker:</strong> F</li>
  <li><strong>Interact:</strong> E</li>
  <li><strong>Pause:</strong> Esc</li>
</ul>

<h2>🖼️ Art &amp; Sound</h2>
<p>
  Pixel art visuals built on the <strong>Comfy52 palette</strong>. The mine shifts from
  warm soil browns at the surface through cool stone greys and glowing copper veins to
  deep purples and cyan gem formations near the bottom. Cozy ambient tracks keep the
  mood relaxed — until a boss appears.
</p>

<h2>📦 Made With</h2>
<p>Godot 4.5 &nbsp;|&nbsp; Solo / small-team indie project &nbsp;|&nbsp; $3–5</p>

<p>
  <em>
    Pickaxe Pioneer is currently in active development.
    Wishlist or follow to be notified at launch!
  </em>
</p>
```
