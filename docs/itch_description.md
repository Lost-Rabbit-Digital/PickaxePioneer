## Full Description (HTML for itch.io editor)

```html
<p>
  Your father ran these mining routes for thirty years — a one-cat operation, a battered ship,
  and a reputation for always coming back up alive. Then he didn't.
</p>
<p>
  Now his ship is yours, his routes are yours, and so are his debts to the Clowder.
  Pickaxe Pioneer is a 2D mining roguelite about keeping a family business alive one planet at a time.
  Dig deep into procedurally generated alien worlds, bank your ore before your energy gives out,
  and spend your haul on upgrades that let you go a little deeper next run.
  The Clowder needs minerals to fuel its next jump. You need to prove you're the cat your father was.
</p>
<p>
  
    If you lost hours to Motherload wondering what's deeper and the thrill of running out of fuel near the surface,
    or you love the chill between-run progression of Coal LLC Pickaxe Pioneer is built for you.
  
</p>
<h2>The Loop</h2>
<p>
  Choose a mine from your overworld warren. Descend into a procedurally generated 128-row shaft.
  Mine ore with your cursor &mdash; richer veins wait deeper, but every underground tile costs a
  heartbeat of stamina. Bank your haul by reaching the surface Exit Station.
  Run out of energy or HP? Lose everything you dug up. Spend what you've banked on permanent
  Clowder upgrades. Head back down &mdash; a little deeper this time.
</p>
<h2>Features</h2>
<ul>
  <li>
    <strong>Procedural mines</strong> &mdash; every run is unique, with depth-weighted ore distribution,
    open cave rooms with ore-rich walls, hazard clusters, and energy nodes scattered throughout
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Five boss encounters</strong> &mdash; from the Giant Rat King at row 32 to the Ancient Hound
    in the deepest caverns at row 128, each defeated using your existing mining tools &mdash;
    no separate combat system
  </li><li>
    <strong>Scout Cat companion</strong> &mdash; your forager automatically collects 40% of nearby ore
    and banks it directly when full, safe from death &mdash; a tiny insurance policy on every risky dive</li></ul>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Galaxy Map</strong>&mdash; navigate a planet-based map between mines of varying depth,
    richness, and danger.</li>
</ul>
<h2>Early Access</h2>
<p>
  Pickaxe Pioneer is in active early access.
  Core gameplay is playable now.
  More mines, fossils, run modifiers, and various other&nbsp;content is in progress. your feedback shapes what gets built next.
</p>
```
